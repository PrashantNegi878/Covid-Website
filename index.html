Prevention from Corona Virus
Stay Home, Stay Safe
How To Prevent Virus

wash your place

Cleaning with a household cleaner that contains soap or detergent reduces the amount of germs on surfaces and decreases risk of infection from surfaces.

maintain distance

Maintain at least 1 metre distance between yourself and anyone who is coughing or sneezing. If you are too close, you can breathe in the droplets, including the COVID-19 virus if the person coughing has the disease.

don't touch face

Hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses. Once contaminated, hands can transfer the virus to your eyes, nose or mouth. From there, the virus can enter your body and can make you sick.

wash your hand

Washing your hands with soap and water or using alcohol-based hand rub kills viruses that may be on your hands.

use napkin

Napkins come in handy when you need to cough or sneeze at the table. Cover your mouth and nose with your napkin to help keep germs contained.

wear a mask

When you wear a mask, you protect others as well as yourself. Masks work best when everyone wears one.
